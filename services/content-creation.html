Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our content creation services.

How long does a typical content shoot take?

A standard content shoot takes 2.5-3 hours on location. This includes setup time, capturing a variety of shots, and ensuring we have all the content you need. For more complex shoots or those requiring multiple locations, we may recommend a longer session.

How many photos and videos do I get from each shoot?

The number of deliverables varies by package. Our monthly package includes 15 edited photos and 3 short videos, while our one-off and quarterly packages include 10 edited photos and 2 short videos. We can customize these numbers based on your specific needs.

Do I need to provide props or styling for the shoot?

We'll discuss all requirements during the pre-shoot planning session. In most cases, we'll use your existing products, props, and styling. For specialized shoots, we may recommend additional props or styling elements to enhance the final results.

How long does it take to receive the edited content?

We typically deliver edited content within 5-7 business days after the shoot. For clients on our monthly package, we prioritize their content to ensure consistent posting schedules. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee if needed.

Can you help with content strategy beyond just creation?

Absolutely! We offer add-on services including content calendar creation, strategy sessions, and analytics breakdown. These services help ensure your content aligns with your marketing goals and performs well on your chosen platforms.

Do you offer video editing services separately?

Yes, we can provide video editing services for content you've already captured. This is perfect for businesses that have their own in-house content creation but need professional editing to elevate their final product.
